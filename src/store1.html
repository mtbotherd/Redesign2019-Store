Add value to card

Add value to an existing Go-To Card, Metropass, College Pass, U-Pass or Student Pass.

Please allow 24 hours for value to be available on your card. Need it in fewer than 24 hours? Visit an authorized fare retailer. All sales are final.

Where is the serial number?

Your Go-To Card serial number is a 16-digit number found in the lower-right corner on the back of your card.

 

Visitor Pass

$5

Unlimited rides until 2 a.m. after first use. Visitor Passes are valid for a $2.50 fare (cannot be used on Northstar, express buses or Metro Mobility). Also available at Transit Service Centers.

This pass will be shipped within FIVE business days. Learn more at metrotransit.org/visitor-pass.
